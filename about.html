About AdoPay

AdoPay is a fintech startup formed in Arusha Tanzania by bachelor students. We are basically aiming at providing reliable, cheap, and secured mobile payment services across the region.
Right now AdoPay is in process of developing a prototype of the solution.
We are expecting to launch as early as possible.

How We Started.

Fintech is the outgrowing industry in this fourth industrial revolution, The need for reliable, affordable and secured systems is innevitable.
In Tanzania, mobile payment services are provided by mobile network companies,
The government Tax, fees, tariffs and charges in addition to service charges offered by service providers are very high which led to the decline of the industry.
Here is where we Started, we design a very cheap solution for payment services.
We believe there must be a specialized entity for mobile payments services only.

Why we created AdoPay

People needs to have a single application that manage all of their payments,
People need cheap, secured and reliable payments services, we want every one to enjoy paying online.
Quality services is the right of every person.

Our Mission

To become the your company of choice substituting your physical payments and wallets with smart mobile wallet and digital payment which is innovative, secure, & user friendly

Our Objective

Allowing customers to pay for goods and services with their smartphones instead of cash or credit cards, and Increase knowledge to our customers about mobile payment
